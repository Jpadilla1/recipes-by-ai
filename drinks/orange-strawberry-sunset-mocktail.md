# Orange Strawberry Sunset Mocktail

<div class="recipe-meta">
**Prep Time:** 5 mins
**Total Time:** 5 mins
**Yield:** 2 servings
</div>

A refreshing layered mocktail with vibrant strawberry and orange flavors, perfect for any occasion.

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup strawberries (fresh or frozen)
- 1 tablespoon maple syrup (or honey/agave)
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1 cup orange juice (freshly squeezed if possible)
- 1/2 cup sparkling water (or lemon-lime soda for a sweeter version)
- Ice cubes

### Optional Garnishes

- Orange slices
- Strawberry slices
- Mint leaves

## Instructions

1. **Make the strawberry base:** Blend strawberries with lemon juice and maple syrup until smooth. Strain if you want a smoother texture.
2. **Prepare glasses:** Fill each glass with ice.
3. **Layer the strawberry:** Pour the strawberry mixture into the bottom of each glass.
4. **Add orange juice:** Slowly pour in orange juice to create a gradient effect.
5. **Top with sparkling water:** Pour sparkling water gently over the back of a spoon to preserve layers.
6. **Garnish and serve:** Add a slice of orange, a strawberry on the rim, or a mint sprig for flair.
