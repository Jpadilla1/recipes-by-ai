# Honey Baked Pears

<div class="recipe-meta">
**Prep Time:** 10 mins
**Cook Time:** 30 mins
**Total Time:** 40 mins
**Yield:** 4 servings
</div>

Simple and elegant baked pears with honey, butter, and warm spices - perfect served with yogurt, whipped cream, or ice cream.

## Ingredients

- 4 ripe but firm pears, halved and cored
- 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
- 2 tablespoons honey or maple syrup
- ¼ teaspoon ground clove or nutmeg (optional)
- Pinch of salt
- ¼ cup chopped nuts (walnuts, pecans, hazelnuts, or pistachios) – optional
- Vanilla yogurt, whipped cream, or ice cream for serving

## Instructions

1. **Heat oven:** Heat oven to 375°F (190°C).
2. **Prepare pears:** Halve pears lengthwise and remove the cores.
3. **Arrange in dish:** Place pears cut-side up in a baking dish.
4. **Add butter and honey:** Brush each pear with melted butter and drizzle with honey.
5. **Season:** Sprinkle lightly with clove or nutmeg if using, plus a pinch of salt.
6. **Add nuts (optional):** Add nuts to the hollowed centers if desired.
7. **Bake:** Bake 25–30 minutes, until pears are tender and lightly caramelized.
8. **Finish:** Spoon the warm pan juices over the pears before serving.
9. **Serve:** Serve warm with yogurt, whipped cream, or ice cream.
