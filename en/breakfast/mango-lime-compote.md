# Mango-Lime Compote

**Prep Time:** 5 mins
**Cook Time:** 5 mins
**Total Time:** 10 mins
**Yield:** 4 servings

A bright, fruity compote of simmered mango with honey and lime — perfect spooned generously over pancakes while warm.

## Ingredients

- 1 mango, diced into 1cm cubes
- 1 tablespoon lime juice
- 1 tablespoon honey
- ½ teaspoon lime zest
- 1 pinch salt

## Instructions

1. **Combine:** Add the diced mango, honey, lime juice, and salt to a small saucepan over medium heat. Stir to combine.
2. **Simmer:** Let it simmer for about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the mango softens and the liquid thickens into a light syrup. Some chunks should still hold their shape.
3. **Finish:** Remove from heat, stir in the lime zest, and taste — add a tiny bit more honey if the lime is too tart.
4. **Serve:** Spoon generously over pancakes while warm.

## Tips

- Start with half the lime juice if your lime is very tart and add more at the end.
- For a chunkier texture, add half the mango at the start and fold in the rest at the end.
- A splash of coconut cream on top takes it up a notch.
